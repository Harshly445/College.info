1) College of Engineering, Pune




About COEP Pune

College of Engineering, Pune, established in 1854, is a globally known pioneer in the field of technical education. The Institute is defined by its dedication to seeking solutions to today's major problems through technological advancements. The organisation has a rich history and is committed to the achievement of excellence. COEP provides a unique learning environment along with a variety of academic activities. With a firm foundation of reality and humanity, the college offers one an appreciation of both the scientific innovations and the values that go with them. The programme is structured to improve your educational qualifications through resources such as work placements, study abroad opportunities and academic institutions. The strength of COEP education is its large and diverse network of graduates, industry support and togetherness, which the institute shares with many international universities. The college has been ranked 52nd in the NIRF Rankings of 2021 in the engineering category.

Location

COEP is located at Wellesley Road, Shivajinagar, Pune, Maharashtra. College of Engineering, Pune, is around 9.5 km away from the Pune Airport (PNQ). The Pune station is just 2.5 km far from COEP and Shivaji Nagar is in the area of the campus.

2) Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering




About BVUCOE Pune

Established in the year 1983, the Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering, Pune has been an institution synonymous with delivering high-quality education and all-round development to students through the years. The institute is acclaimed as one of the best engineering colleges in the area. The institute was first affiliated to the Pune university till 2000, after which on the recommendation of UGC and AICTE, the college got affiliated to the Bharati Vidyapeeth University. The university allotts degrees that are equivalent to central and state statutory universities. The institute has a well equipped campus with amenities for advanced learning and growth of students through various lectures and other events and activities. The campus premises consist of facilities such as Hostels, advanced classrooms, Wifi, Cafeteria, sports ground and equipment, hospitals, gym, central Library and audio visual hall etc. The Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering, Pune has all the facilities to make the life of students and staff comfortable on the campus. The institute has an excellent record of placement with rising packages each year. The Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University College of Engineering, Pune was ranked 99th by NIRF in the Engineering category rankings of 2020. The institute has consistently improved on the national rankings and has become a name of prestige in the recent past.

3)Vishwakarma Institute of Technology




About VIT Pune

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT Pune) was established in 1983. VIT Pune is an autonomous institution affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. VIT Pune is run by the Bansilal Ramnath Agarwal Charitable Trust, Pune. The institute is spread over a campus size of 18 acres. VIT Pune has a total faculty of 242 members. The college offers a total of 25 courses at the undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels. The streams in which these engineering courses are offered are- Chemical Engineering, Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Industrial & Production Engineering, Instrumentation Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering. VIT Pune offers a variety of facilities and infrastructure to its students. The institute offers scholarships to meritorious and needy students. The college also provides placement opportunities to students. As per the NIRF ranking 2021, VIT Pune has been ranked 122 among engineering institutions with an overall score of 36.82. This ranking is based on various parameters such as graduation outcomes, teaching, learning, and resources, perception, outreach and inclusivity, research and professional practice. The vision of the institute is to become a worldwide recognised institute for technical education and research that promotes holistic socio-economic development. The mission of VIT Pune is to ensure that all students are employable and work in industries, higher education, become entrepreneurs, or get civil/defense services/government jobs, and other fields such as sports and theatre.

4)Symbiosis Institute of Technology




About SIT Pune

Established in 2008, Symbiosis Institute of Technology is a constituent of Symbiosis International (Deemed University). The institute offers B.Tech in Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning and Robotics & Automation and M.Tech programmes in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Robotics & Automation and Embedded Systems. SIT Pune mission is to inculcate the spirit of 'Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam' (the world is one family) and to promote ethical and value-based learning. The vision of SIT Pune is to promote international understanding through quality education. SIT Pune has collaborated with foreign institutions such as Nanyang Technological University Singapore, Ingolstadt University Germany, Purdue School of Engineering, IUPUI Indianapolis USA and Leibniz University Hannover, King Mongkut’s University, Thailand, University of Malaya, Malaysia, Ritsumeikan University, Japan, Hanyang University, South Korea etc. The institute has a campus area of 16 acres, located in Tal Mushi, Pune.

5)Army Institute of Technology




About AIT Pune

Army Institute of Technology is a private college established in 1994 exclusively for children of serving and retired army personnel. It is also known as AIT Pune. It functions under the aegis of Army Welfare Education Society (AWES). The college is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). The institute is recognised by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). AIT Pune has been accredited by NAAC with an ‘A’ grade. The college campus covers an area of 32 acres. As per the official website, Army Institute of Technology mission is to provide the right environment, to the wards of Defence personnel, for development of physical, intellectual, emotional and spiritual quotients, with a view to produce total quality engineers. The institute offers 4 B.E and one M.E courses. All the undergraduate courses offered by the college are National Board of Accreditation (NBA). JEE Main and GATE scores are accepted for admission to UG and PG courses respectively. AIT Pune has a training and placement cell that looks after the placement of students. Meritorious and students from financially weaker sections of the society are provided with scholarships by the college. AIT Pune campus has a large number of facilities and modern infrastructure for students and faculty members.
